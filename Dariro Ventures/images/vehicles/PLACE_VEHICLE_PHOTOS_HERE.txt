Place your vehicle photos here.

Recommended image sizes:
- Width:  800px minimum
- Height: 500px minimum
- Format: .jpg or .webp (webp is smaller/faster)

Naming convention used in js/data.js:
- sedan.jpg
- van.jpg
- hilux.jpg  (4x4)
- fortuner.jpg (suv)
- executive.jpg
- bus.jpg

To use local images instead of URLs, update the "image" field
in js/data.js from the URL to a relative path, e.g.:
  image: 'images/vehicles/sedan.jpg'
